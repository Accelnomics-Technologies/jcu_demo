Study at JCU Brisbane

Conveniently located in a modern high-rise building in the heart of Brisbane’s CBD, the James Cook University Brisbane (JCUB) you  campus features interactive learning spaces, comfortable student lounges and a resource centre. Mingle with university staff and fellow students from over 88 countries within our vibrant and close-knit community.

As a JCUB student you have access to dedicated student support including opportunities for enhanced job-support services, academic learning advisors, counselling, employment assistance, and English language and academic pathways.

Pathways to JCU

Begin your university studies with confidence through a JCU Pathway.

Prepare for tertiary studies with programs to develop your study skills or boost your capabilities as you prepare for postgraduate qualifications. You'll benefit from supported entry into some specific JCU Brisbane degrees.

Choose from a general higher education-focused certificate to support you in building academic abilities, through to specialised Diploma courses where you'll develop the specific knowledge and skills required for a particular JCU degree, and more. JCU Brisbane is there to empower you to get where you want to go, both academically and in your future career.

Discover JCU's Pathway courses today

Experience Queensland's capital

Brisbane is the capital of Queensland and the third largest city in Australia with a city population of 1.2 million. It's also one of the most affordable cities in Australia, and is known for being safe and friendly.
With its subtropical climate, good lifestyle, picturesque river precinct, modern shopping, cultural and entertainment options, Brisbane is one of Australia's most liveable cities.
